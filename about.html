Why Kimyon?

Small seed.

Kimyon is the Turkish word for cumin — a humble spice found in kitchens all over the world. Small, unassuming, but always there when it counts. We borrowed the name because it felt true to who we are: a small, dedicated team that takes its work seriously. No flash, just the kind of quiet reliability that makes a real difference.
